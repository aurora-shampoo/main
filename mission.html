Our Mission

Our shampoo is inspired by the natural beauty of the aurora borealis. The aurora borealis is a natural light show, seen at the poles of the earth. The aurora borealis is caused by radiation from the earth magnetic rays, and it protects us from solar rays. Like the Aurora borealis, our shampoo protects us too, from toxic chemicals. Aurora shampoo. Radiating beauty by blocking out dangerous chemicals.
